Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

DarkWall is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide you with the best wallpaper experience. This may include device information (such as device model, operating system, and app version), basic usage data (like which wallpapers are viewed or downloaded), analytics to help improve app performance, and crash reports to ensure app stability.

If you choose to sign up or subscribe, we may collect your email address or account information, but we do not collect any personal information unless you voluntarily provide it.

The information we collect is used solely to enhance your experience with DarkWall and to ensure the smooth functioning of the app. We use analytics data to understand which wallpapers are most popular, improve our content offerings, optimize app performance, and fix bugs.

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing or advertising. Your information is shared only with partners needed to provide DarkWall’s features, and never for unrelated marketing purposes.

Your Choices & Rights

You may choose not to provide certain information or may revoke device permissions at any time. If you have an account, you may request deletion of your information by contacting us at support@darkwall.app.
